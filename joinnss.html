NSS Main Group

The NSS main group, or National Service Scheme main group, is a voluntary public service program in India aimed at developing student volunteers into socially responsible citizens. NSS engages students in community service activities, fostering a sense of empathy, leadership, and civic responsibility. Participants contribute to various social causes, promoting community development and addressing societal issues.
