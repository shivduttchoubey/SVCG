Our Guide

Meet Our Guide – the compass leading us on our journey towards positive change. Discover the insights, wisdom, and expertise that shape our mission. Learn about the guiding principles, values, and vision that steer us in our endeavors. Whether you're a new member or a long-time supporter, this page provides a glimpse into the leadership and guidance that propels our collective efforts. Join us on this inspiring journey as we navigate the path towards a brighter and more compassionate future, guided by our shared commitment to service and community upliftment.

Message From Principal Dear Students, As Principal of SVCG Government Science College, I am proud of our Career Guidance Cell's commitment to student success. In today's competitive world, career guidance is not just an option – it's a necessity for building a strong professional foundation. Our Career Guidance Cell exemplifies excellence through its dedicated team, industry connections, and comprehensive support system. We have consistently helped our students secure promising opportunities across various sectors, making our institution a stepping stone to successful careers. I encourage each student to take full advantage of this valuable resource. Your growth and success are central to our institution's mission.

Dr.

Jaya Bajpai
Incharge SVCG Cell,
Government Science College, Jabalpur

As the Incharge of SVCG's Career Guidance Cell, I am committed to helping you build successful careers. Our mission is to transform academic excellence into professional achievement through proper guidance and support. The Career Guidance Cell serves as your platform for career development, offering personalized counseling, skill enhancement workshops, and placement assistance. We believe in nurturing individual potential and helping students make informed career choices. I invite you to utilize our resources and remember that my door is always open for those seeking guidance. Together, we can turn your career aspirations into reality.
