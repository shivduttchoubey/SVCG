Embracing Unity and Service
Two Years of Success with NSS Parade

NSS Parade has been a successful tradition in our college since the year 2020. Celebrating the spirit of unity and service, the NSS Parade on January 26th has become a significant event, marked by two years of accomplishment.
Volunteers from all academic years will actively participate in this event, contributing to the spirit of community service and fostering a sense of pride and responsibility.

Empowering Communities
Reflecting on an Impactful NSS Camp in Ghana

An enriching NSS camp recently took place in Ghana, where dedicated volunteers from various backgrounds came together for a week of impactful community service. Set against the backdrop of Ghana's vibrant culture and warm hospitality, the camp focused on addressing problems faced by local community.
Participants engaged in a range of activities, including educational initiatives, health awareness campaigns, and environmental projects. The camaraderie and shared sense of purpose among the NSS volunteers created a memorable experience, fostering not only personal growth but also leaving a positive impact on the communities served. The NSS camp in Ghana exemplified the spirit of volunteerism and international cooperation, leaving lasting impressions on both the volunteers and the local residents.
