Thought by Swami Vivekanand

When you give people responsibility for improving their lives - when you instill in them self-confidence, without which they are nothing. When they develop self-confidence, they can solve even the most difficult situations and to awaken this self-confidence in them, it is necessary that you have full faith in their power of nation building. Trust breeds confidence and democracy flourishes when the entire public, especially the weaker sections of the society, completely accept the facts.

Pushparaj Saket

Being an SVCG volunteer has been a transformative journey, allowing me to contribute meaningfully to society. The experiences gained during various community projects have not only broadened my perspective but also instilled a profound sense of responsibility towards community welfare.

Sharad Shrivastava

Participating in SVCG activities has been more than just volunteering; it's a journey of self-discovery and empathy. The impact we create together as a team transcends individual efforts, making every moment spent in service truly rewarding.

Chhaya Khushwaha

The joy of helping others and the sense of fulfillment derived from community service have become an integral part of my identity.
