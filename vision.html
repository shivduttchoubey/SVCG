National Service Scheme (NSS)

Guiding Principles for Positive Change

Vision

The vision of the National Service Scheme (NSS) is to build a society where every individual is motivated and equipped to contribute towards the well-being of the community. NSS envisions a world where young minds actively engage in service, fostering a sense of social responsibility and compassion.

Aim

The aim of NSS is to instill the spirit of voluntary community service and develop a sense of civic and social responsibility among students. NSS strives to create a platform for young individuals to actively participate in community development, gain practical knowledge, and contribute towards building a just and inclusive society.

Purpose

The purpose of NSS is multifaceted. It includes providing opportunities for students to work with communities, addressing local issues, promoting national integration, and developing leadership and organizational skills. NSS endeavors to create a generation of socially conscious leaders who understand the importance of service in nation-building and global harmony.
