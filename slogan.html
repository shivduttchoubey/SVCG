National Service Scheme (NSS)

Inspiring Thoughts and Guiding Slogan

Thought

The guiding thought of the National Service Scheme (NSS) revolves around the belief that the development of an individual is intricately linked with the progress of society. NSS encourages the notion that through selfless service, individuals not only contribute to the welfare of the community but also undergo personal growth, developing into responsible and empathetic citizens.
